Our Choosen Charity: Treedom

Treedom collaborates with small farmers all over the world to help them integrate a more sustainable agricultural system - Agroforestry.

What is Agroforestry?

Agroforestry is a Sustainable Agricultural Practice that also provides Food Security for the farmers and their communities boosting their economical development.

How do we work with them?

Every month, we will donate the reward generated by the pool and exchange it for trees to be planted around the world. These trees will be issued an ID that will allow our delegators to track them and their development.

Why Treedom?

We chose this project because of their impact - not only ecological - but within the communities being supported by this initiative. Also due to its traceability , that allow us to have maximum transparency with our delegators.

Each tree will then be planted and will be traceable - thanks to their ID system that allows you to have information on:

  • Where the tree has been planted
  • Who is the farmer that is caring for the tree
  • How is it doing - with an up-to-date photo tag.

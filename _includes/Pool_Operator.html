Treedom

What is Treedom?

Treedom is an organisation that collaborates with small farmers all over the world to help them integrate a more sustainable agricultural system - Agroforestry.

Agroforestry consists in cultivating native species integrated with the ecosystem where they are farmed.

Treedom educates farmers in these best agricultural practices, while supporting them through the 'growth phase' (while the trees are not yet productive).

This results in a sustainable economical development for the farmers and their communities. Ensuring that our investement in trees plantation will 'bear fruits' and capture CO2 for generations!

How do we work with them?

Treedom has a website where people can buy trees.

Those will then be planted and will be traceable, thanks to their ID system that allows you to have information:

  • where the tree has been planted
  • information about the farmer that is caring for that tree
  • even an updated photo tag.

    • We chose this project because of their impact - not only ecological - but within the communities being supported by this initiative. Also due to its traceability - we wanted to be able to trace and see our investement actually grow and give fruits.

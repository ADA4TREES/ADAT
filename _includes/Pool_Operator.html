Treedom

What is Treedom?

Treedom is an organisation that collaborates with small farmers all over the world to help them integrate a more sustainable agricultural system - Agroforestry. Agroforestry consists in cultivating native species integrated with the ecosystem where they are farmed. They do so by educating the small farmers and the communities in best agricultural practices and also by supporting their income during the growth phase of the plants. While they are not yet productive.

How do we work with them?

Treedom has a website where people can buy trees. Those will then be planted and will be traceable, thanks to their ID system that allows you to have information of where the tree has been planted, information about the farmer that is caring for that tree and even an updated photo tag.

We chose this project because of their impact - not only ecological - but within the communities being supported by this initiative. Also due to its traceability - we wanted to be able to trace and see our investement actually grow and give fruits.
